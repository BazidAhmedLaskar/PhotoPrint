💡 Quick Tips

For Best Results

  1. Start with Exposure to fix lighting
  2. Adjust Brightness and Contrast
  3. Increase Saturation for vibrancy
  4. Add subtle Sharpness (0-30%)
  5. Export in High Quality for best results

Passport Photo Shortcut

  1. Crop image to face
  2. Click "📷 Passport Photo"
  3. Select layout (4x6, 6x8, or 8x12)
  4. Download PNG
  5. Print A4 ready-to-cut photos

Image Compression Tip

  1. Click "📦 Compress"
  2. Enter target size (e.g., 200 KB)
  3. Wait for optimization
  4. Download optimized image
  5. Perfect for email or form uploads
